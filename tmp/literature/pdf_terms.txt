[Abstract] page 7: Abstract: Textual research materials and forms are interdependent in document textual research. The former provides documentary evidence for the latter, while the latter provides structure and principles for the former. Chuang Tzu Za Zhi uses textual materials such as dictionaries, ancient notes and examples to support the arguments from the perspective of the textual research forms, and uses the forms such as seeking meaning from sounds, rhymes, shape of characters, correspondence, and regulations of ancient notes to endow the materials with life. Rich textual research experience and strong desire of “getting the true meaning of the texts” encouraged “the Two WANGs of Gaoyou” to “explain words by meaning” without ample textual research material or forms. To conclude, rich textual research materials, self-conscious textual research forms, factual research spirit, and the purpose of “getting the true meaning of the texts” have all contributed to a prominent position in the history of textual research for “the Two Wangs of Gaoyou”.
